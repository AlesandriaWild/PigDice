The rules of Pig Dice are:
  1. Each turn a player rolls the die, until the play rolls a 1 or player "holds". If a play rolls a 1, no score is given to the player for that round.
  2. If the player rolls any number 2-6 it will be added to the players total score.
  3. To pass the turn to the next player, the current player needs to "hold" their turn, or roll a 1.
  4. The first player to 100 wins the game!
